## Notes:
- An axon is a single, tubelike, extension that transmits neural information away from the soma to other neurons or cells.
- Most neurons only have one axon, but many axons have branches that allow a single message to be sent to multiple cells.
## Questions:

Q: What is an axon?
A: A single tubelike extension of a neuron that transmits neural information away from the soma towards other neurons or cells.
